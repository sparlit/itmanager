## 2. Homepage Structure & Rationale

1.  **Hero Section:** Instant value prop. Communicates "What we do" in <8s.
2.  **Trust/Press Section:** Logos of partner brands or certifications to borrow authority.
3.  **Core Services:** Visual selection of 4 main service pillars.
4.  **How It Works:** Simplifies the process (Pickup → Clean → Delivery).
5.  **Social Proof:** 5 high-impact testimonials to reduce perceived risk.
6.  **FAQ:** Proactively answers common objections (Pricing, Stains, Turnaround).
7.  **Final CTA:** Removes all remaining friction for immediate action.

## 5. About Page Copy

**Origin Story:** Pristine was born from a simple frustration: why was it so hard to find a reliable laundry in Doha that cared as much about fabrics as the owners did? We started in 2018 with one machine and a mission to treat every garment like a piece of art. Today, we are Qatar’s most trusted fabric care specialists.

**Vision:** To redefine laundry as a luxury experience, making garment care effortless for every resident and business in Qatar.
**Mission:** Combining master craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to deliver the world's most reliable and high-quality laundry service.

**Core Values:**
1.  **Integrity:** We treat your clothes and your time with absolute respect.
2.  **Innovation:** We use the world's best FOSS technology to provide a seamless digital experience.
3.  **Sustainability:** We use 40% less water and 100% biodegradable detergents to protect Qatar's environment.

**The Pristine Difference:** Unlike others who outsource their cleaning, we own our 10,000 sq. ft. facility. Every item is tracked via RFID, photographed on arrival, and inspected twice by our Quality Control masters before it leaves our floor.
