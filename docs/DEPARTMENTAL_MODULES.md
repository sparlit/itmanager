# Departmental Portal Modules
## 15+ Specialized ERP Interfaces

Each department is designed as a standalone-capable module connected to the central PostgreSQL database.

---

### 1. Administration (HQ Command)
*   **Executive Dashboard:** Real-time revenue, order volume, and departmental KPIs.
*   **Global Audit Log:** Track every action taken across all portals.
*   **System Config:** Manage branch settings, service types, and global pricing.
*   **Crisis Management:** Master override for machine or fleet shutdowns.

### 2. Sales (B2B & Growth)
*   **Lead CRM:** Track inquiries from hotels, spas, and clinics.
*   **Contract Manager:** Manage B2B SLAs, bulk pricing, and renewal dates.
*   **Commission Tracker:** Real-time earnings for sales agents.
*   **Proposals Engine:** Generate professional PDFs for corporate bids.

### 3. Marketing (Brand & Loyalty)
*   **Campaign Builder:** Launch WhatsApp/SMS/Email promos.
*   **Loyalty Engine:** Configure reward tiers and referral bonuses.
*   **Review Manager:** Monitor and reply to Google/Social reviews.
*   **Asset Library:** Access brand logos, photos, and ad templates.

### 4. Customer Service (Support)
*   **Unified Inbox:** One view for WhatsApp, Phone, and Email tickets.
*   **Complaint Matrix:** Automated escalation if a ticket isn't resolved in 12h.
*   **Order Rescheduling:** Quick-access tools for changing pickup times.
*   **Knowledge Base:** FAQ scripts and fabric care guides for agents.

### 5. Production (The Factory Floor)
*   **Sorting Queue:** Intake view with AI-Vision item counting.
*   **Machine Monitoring:** Real-time status of washers/dryers (MQTT).
*   **QC Log:** Photo evidence capture for arrival condition & final result.
*   **Chemical Dosing:** Automated detergent usage tracking.

### 6. Operations (Resource Planning)
*   **Staff Scheduling:** Rota management for factory and drivers.
*   **Capacity Planner:** Forecast bottlenecks based on current order volume.
*   **Facility Logs:** Utility tracking (Water/Energy usage).
*   **Incident Reports:** Log workplace safety or process errors.

### 7. Transport (Logistics)
*   **Route Optimizer:** TSP-based nearest-neighbor mapping for drivers.
*   **Dual-Scan Custody:** Handover verification (Driver ↔ Factory).
*   **Fuel/Expensing:** Receipt capture for petrol and parking.
*   **Proof of Attempt:** GPS-verified photo evidence for missed pickups.

### 8. Human Resource (Staffing)
*   **Employee Directory:** Digital files for all 500+ staff.
*   **Payroll (WPS):** Automated generation of Qatar SIF files.
*   **Recruitment:** Applicant tracking and interview scheduling.
*   **Training Sandbox:** Onboarding modules for new system users.

### 9. Information Technology (SysAdmin)
*   **Health Monitor:** API latency, DB load, and server uptime.
*   **Asset Register:** RFID tracking for all company laptops/phones.
*   **Security Console:** MFA management and session force-logout.
*   **Backup Manager:** PITR PostgreSQL restore control.

### 10. Maintenance (Hardware)
*   **Machine PM:** Preventive maintenance schedule for washers.
*   **Spare Parts Inventory:** Stock tracking for belts, motors, and filters.
*   **Breakdown Tickets:** Urgent repair requests from Production.
*   **Vendor Links:** Quick-dial for external machine manufacturers.

### 11. Finance (The Ledger)
*   **AR/AP:** Accounts Receivable and Payable tracking.
*   **COD Reconciliation:** Matching driver cash collected to order data.
*   **ISO 14001 Reporting:** Automated "Green" sustainability reports.
*   **Tax/Audit:** Ready-to-export files for Qatari tax authorities.

### 12. Business Development (Partnerships)
*   **Joint Venture Tracker:** Manage partnerships with supermarkets/malls.
*   **Market Intelligence:** Competitor pricing and area demand heatmaps.
*   **Expansion Planner:** Tools for analyzing new branch locations.

### 13. Communications (PR)
*   **Press Room:** Draft and distribute media releases.
*   **Internal Comms:** Company-wide announcements and shift notes.
*   **Crisis Comms:** Pre-approved templates for system-wide alerts.

### 14. Security (Asset Protection)
*   **CCTV Bridge:** Integration with factory/branch camera feeds.
*   **Access Control:** Log of staff entries to sensitive areas.
*   **Incident Log:** Theft, damage, or unauthorized entry reports.

### 15. Housekeeping (Hygiene)
*   **Cleanliness Log:** Checklist for branch/factory hygiene standards.
*   **Supply Tracker:** Inventory of detergents, mops, and hygiene chemicals.
*   **Shift Handover:** Notes on facility condition between shifts.
