================================================================================
1. ARCHITECTURAL PHILOSOPHY
================================================================================
The system is built as a "Federated Monorepo"—a collection of 17+ standalone-style web applications (1 Public E-Com, 1 B2B Dashboard, 15 Internal Departments) that share a common "Heart" (Database & Logic) but possess unique "Skins" (UIs) and "Brains" (AI Agents).

Core Principles:
- **Plug-and-Play:** Modules are fully decoupled. Adding or removing a department portal has zero effect on others.
- **Offline-First (PWA):** Critical production and transport apps work without internet, syncing via background workers.
- **Privacy-by-Design:** Qatar Law No. 13 compliance through field-level encryption.
- **UX Grammar:** Standardized functional positioning (icons, buttons, alerts) across all 15 distinct visual themes.

================================================================================
2. TECHNOLOGY STACK (100% FOSS)
================================================================================
- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router) with PWA support (Serwist/Next-PWA).
- **Language:** TypeScript (Strict Mode).
- **Database:** PostgreSQL + Prisma with Field-Level Encryption Middleware.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS 4 + Framer Motion (Sci-Fi & Luxury animations).
- **Maps:** Leaflet.js + OpenStreetMap + OSRM (Open Source Routing Machine).
- **AI Agents:** LangGraph & CrewAI (adapted from 500-AI-Agents-Projects).
- **Computer Vision:** YOLOv8 (FOSS) for AI Stain Detection via WebGL.
- **State:** Zustand with Persistence (for offline states).

5. CRITICAL ENHANCEMENTS & LOOP-HOLE PATCHES
================================================================================
- **Procurement Module:** Integrated into 'Maintenance' and 'Operations' to track chemical/detergent levels. Prevents "Supply Bottlenecks."
- **Privacy Layer:** Middleware that encrypts 'phone' and 'address' fields in Postgres. Only authorized portals (CS, Transport, Admin) hold the decryption keys.
- **UX Grammar:** A shared design language where:
    - Primary actions are always Bottom-Right.
    - Global notifications are always Top-Center.
    - System health is always Bottom-Left.
- **B2B White-Labeling:** The `b2b-dashboard` app allows corporate clients (e.g., Hilton) to see their own logos and high-volume contract data.

================================================================================
6. QATAR LOCALIZATION & COMPLIANCE
================================================================================
- **Address System:** Native support for Qatar Building-Street-Zone identifiers.
- **Language:** Dual-path routing (English/Arabic) with RTL support in the Public Portal.
- **Currency:** QAR-first with local bank gateway abstraction.
- **Traffic Intelligence:** Custom AI agent using OpenStreetMap data to predict congestion in Doha zones during peak hours.
