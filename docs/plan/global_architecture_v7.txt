================================================================================
3. FEDERATED MONOREPO ENHANCEMENTS (v7.0)
================================================================================

### A. ARRIVAL CONDITION CAPTURE (Visual Proof)
- **Rule:** For items > 1,000 QAR, the Production portal forces 3 timestamped photos before the wash cycle is authorized.
- **Visibility:** Photos are immediately available in the customer's "Track Order" portal to prevent damage disputes.

### B. CHEMICAL & ENERGY INTERLOCKS
- **IoT Safety:** Washing machines will not start unless the detergent barcode scan matches the Fabric Type (Silk/Cotton) in the DB.
- **Monitoring:** Maintenance portal tracks real-time kWh per machine, flagging mechanical drift.

### C. TRANSLATION MANAGEMENT (Weblate)
- **Dynamic Arabic:** Admin portal features a direct JSON editor for Arabic labels, allowing instant updates to terminology without code deployment.

### D. DIGITAL WARDROBE & PREFERENCES
- **Intelligence:** System remembers "Fold don't hang" or "Heavy Starch" per garment type (Cotton Shirt vs. Silk Abaya).

### E. COLD STORAGE PIPELINE
- **Optimization:** Orders older than 2 years are moved to Parquet files. This keeps the primary database lean and maintains sub-100ms query times during peak Ramadan traffic.
