## SECTION 1: COMPLETE WEBSITE ARCHITECTURE & SITE PLAN

### 1.1 The Federated Monorepo Model
The system is architected as a **Federated Monorepo** (Source: Turborepo / [turborepo.org](https://turbo.build/)). This allows 18+ standalone-style web applications to share a common PostgreSQL backbone while remaining visually and functionally isolated.

### 1.2 Site Map & Portal Definitions
1.  **Public E-Commerce Portal:** High-end customer interface for order booking, tracking, and subscription management.
2.  **B2B Corporate Dashboard:** White-labeled portal for hospitality and medical partners to manage high-volume contracts.
3.  **Vendor Portal:** Self-service interface for supply-chain partners to acknowledge POs and upload invoices.
4.  **15 Specialized Internal Portals:**
    *   **Administration:** Global control and auditing.
    *   **IT:** System health and security monitoring (Edex-UI style).
    *   **Finance:** COD reconciliation, QPay sync, and payroll.
    *   **Transport:** OSRM-based route optimization and "Proof of Attempt" logistics.
    *   **Production:** YOLOv8-driven QC and offline PWA scanning.
    *   **Sales/Marketing/CS/HR/Maintenance/Bus-Dev/Comms/Security/Housekeeping.**

### 1.3 Operational Integrity (Zero-Stub Algorithms)
- **Logistics:** Implements a pure TypeScript **Travelling Salesperson Problem (TSP)** nearest-neighbor algorithm for multi-drop efficiency in Doha.
- **Handover:** A **Dual-Scan Protocol** coupled with **Bluetooth LE Proximity Handshaking** ensures zero item loss between field and factory.
