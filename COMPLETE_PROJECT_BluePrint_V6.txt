SECTION 1: COMPLETE WEBSITE PLAN & ARCHITECTURAL BLUEPRINT
==============================================================================

1.1 THE FEDERATED MONOREPO ARCHITECTURE
The system is built as a Federated Monorepo using Next.js 15, PostgreSQL 16, and Prisma. This "Plug-and-Play" architecture hosts 18+ standalone-style web applications that share a common "Heart" (Database & Security) but possess unique "Skins" (UIs) and "Brains" (AI Agents).

1.2 PORTAL DEFINITIONS & SITE MAP
- PUBLIC E-COMMERCE PORTAL: Luxury B2C/B2B interface. Features: Order Scheduling, Subscription Management, Green Dashboard, and Virtual Queue.
- B2B CORPORATE DASHBOARD: White-labeled portal for Hotels/Hospitals. Features: Contract Tracking, Bulk Invoicing, and RFID Asset Discrepancy Reports.
- VENDOR PORTAL: Supply chain interface for detergent and consumable suppliers to manage POs and upload digital invoices.
- 15 DEPARTMENTAL PORTALS: Specialized ERP applications for Administration, IT, Finance, Transport, Production, Sales, Marketing, Customer Service, HR, Maintenance, Business Development, Communications, Security, and Housekeeping.

1.3 MISSION-CRITICAL PROTOCOLS (ZERO-STUB)
- DUAL-SCAN CUSTODY TRANSFER: Items move between Driver and Factory only when both scan the Bag ID within a 10m geofence.
- BLUETOOTH LE HANDSHAKE: Local proximity verification for high-rise Doha towers where GPS drifts.
- CRDT OFFLINE SYNC: Non-destructive merging of offline data from 100+ field workers using LWW-Element-Set logic.
- DATA ARCHIVAL: Automated pipeline moving logs > 90 days to Parquet files in MinIO to maintain PostgreSQL performance.
